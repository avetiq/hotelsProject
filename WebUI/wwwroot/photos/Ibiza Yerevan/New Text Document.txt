Located in Yerevan, Ibiza Hotel offers a sauna with an indoor pool and hammam.

The modern room feature air conditioning, a sofa, DVD player and flat-screen TV with satellite channels. The bathrooms provide a hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers.

There is a shared fully equipped kitchen and room service as well as breakfasts in the room are provided upon request.

Republic Square is 2.5 mi from the property. Yerevan Train Station is 3.8 mi away, and Zvartnots International Airport is 7.1 mi from Ibiza Hotel.
