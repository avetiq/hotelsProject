Located next to the All-Russia Exhibition Center, this hotel offers a spa with a swimming pool, fitness center and massage services. VDNKh Metro Station is 400 m away.

The colorful rooms at the Cosmos Hotel have WiFi access, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, and a refrigerator. Toiletries and a hairdryer are provided in the private bathrooms.

An Russian-style breakfast is served in the Kalinka restaurant. In total, there are 11 different restaurants and cafés at Cosmos Hotel. Guests can enjoy Mediterranean dishes in the Il Gusto restaurant. Chinese meals and karaoke are available in the Empress Hall restaurant.

Moscow city center is a 15-minute metro ride from the hotel.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.7 for a two-person trip.
